Cardio

HIIT + Steady State

HIIT Treadmill

Post-workout 3x/week (Push + Pull days). 1:2 work-to-rest ratio. EPOC effect burns fat 24hrs post-session.

  • 1
    5 min warm-up walk @ 5 km/h
  • 2
    Sprint 30s @ 12–15 km/h
  • 3
    Walk 60s @ 5 km/h
  • 4
    Repeat 10–12 rounds
  • 5
    5 min cool-down @ 4 km/h

12-3-30 Protocol

Post-leg days. Low-impact recovery cardio targeting posterior chain. Protects knees and lower back.

  • 1
    Set treadmill incline to 12%
  • 2
    Speed: 3 mph (4.8 km/h)
  • 3
    Walk for exactly 30 minutes
  • 4
    No holding the rails
  • 5
    Burns ~300–400 kcal/session

Transformation Killers

Refined Sugar

🍺

Alcohol

Halts fat burning. Liver metabolizes ethanol over fat. Causes facial bloating, dehydration, poor sleep quality.
