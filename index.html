Cardio Protocol

HIIT + Steady State

HIIT Treadmill Protocol

Post-workout on 3 days/week (after Push and Pull days). 1:2 work-to-rest ratio. Triggers EPOC — fat burning continues 24hrs post-workout.

  • 1
    5-minute dynamic warm-up walk (3 mph, flat)
  • 2
    Set treadmill: 5–8% incline
  • 3
    Sprint at 85–90% max HR for 30 seconds
  • 4
    Recovery walk at 3–4 mph for 60 seconds
  • 5
    Repeat for 10–12 rounds
  • 6
    5-minute cool-down (weeks 9–12: extend sprints to 45–60 sec)

12-3-30 Protocol

Low-impact recovery cardio for post-leg days. Protects knees and lower back while hitting fat-burning zone. Targets posterior chain.

  • 1
    Set treadmill incline to 12%
  • 2
    Speed at exactly 3 mph
  • 3
    Walk continuously for 30 minutes
  • 4
    Heart rate stays in fat-burning zone throughout
  • 5
    Targets calves, hamstrings, glutes — no joint stress
  • 6
    Do NOT hold the handrails (removes glute engagement)
⚡ Why NOT steady-state cardio? Long slow cardio fails to stimulate muscle preservation and yields minimal metabolic afterburn. HIIT generates EPOC (Excess Post-exercise Oxygen Consumption), keeping your metabolism elevated for up to 24 hours post-workout.

Lifestyle Protocol

Outside the Gym
💧

Daily Hydration

35ml per kg of bodyweight baseline + 500–1000ml extra for training sweat loss.

3.5–4L

Sleep Architecture

Deep slow-wave sleep triggers HGH and testosterone release. Sleep deprivation elevates cortisol — destroys muscle and promotes fat storage.

7–9 Hrs
🧂

Sodium Control (Face Fat)

Excess sodium causes cellular water retention — visible as facial puffiness. Cut processed foods, salted snacks, high-sodium condiments. Increase potassium (leafy greens).

📵

Screen Curfew

Avoid blue-light screens 1 hour before sleep. Blue light suppresses melatonin production, disrupting deep sleep cycles and HGH pulsatile release.

Avoidance Matrix

Transformation Sabotagers
  • Refined Sugars & Liquid Calories Sodas, fruit juices, mithai — cause rapid insulin spikes, immediately shut down lipolysis and promote fat storage.
  • Alcohol Halts fat burning entirely. Liver prioritizes metabolizing ethanol over oxidizing fat. Also causes severe facial bloating and dehydration.
  • Processed Seed Oils & Trans Fats Fried foods in low-quality oils cause systemic inflammation, impair recovery, and add hidden caloric density.
  • Excess Ghee / Heavy Cream Curries Traditional gravies loaded with ghee must switch to dry-roasting, tomato-onion bases thickened with pureed lentils or low-fat yogurt.

Progress Tracking

90-Day Measurement Framework
⚠️ Don't rely on the scale alone. Simultaneous fat loss + muscle gain can cause scale stagnation even when body composition is dramatically improving. Use the full matrix below.
Method Frequency What to Measure Purpose
Scale Weight Weekly Morning, fasted, after restroom — before eating or drinking Rough aggregate trend of total body mass. Do not obsess over daily fluctuations.
Circumference Taping Bi-Weekly Waist (at navel), chest, biceps, mid-thighs Identifies true structural recomposition when scale weight remains static.
Progress Photography Monthly Front, side, back — identical lighting, same time of day Prevents self-perception bias. Visually confirms facial and pectoral fat reduction.
Strength Benchmarking Every 4 Weeks Bench Press, Squat, Deadlift — max working weight for prescribed reps Ensures progressive overload is actively occurring. Strength gains = muscle preservation during deficit.
Daily Habit Tracker Daily Checklist: 3.5L water ✓ 8hrs sleep ✓ 2000 cal ✓ Workout ✓ Elite performers rely on immediate feedback loops. Tracking compliance is the #1 predictor of success.
